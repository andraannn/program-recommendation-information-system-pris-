MSU-IIT Program Recommendation Information System (PRIS)

    The MSU-Iligan Institute of Technology (MSU-IIT) proudly introduces the Program Recommendation Information System (PRIS), an advanced, data-driven tool designed to guide prospective students in selecting academic programs aligned with their unique profiles. Developed by the Center for Computational Analytics and Modelling at PRISM and funded by the Office of the Vice Chancellor for Research and Enterprise (OVCRE), MSU-IIT PRIS is a pioneering initiative aimed at enhancing academic advising through intelligent recommendations.

About the System

    At the forefront of PRIS’s development are Professor Daisy Lou L. Polestico and Associate Professor Johniel E. Babiera, who have applied sophisticated predictive and segmentation modeling techniques to create an intuitive recommendation system. PRIS assesses key aspects of each student’s profile—including high school academic performance, socio-economic background, and the MSU-IIT System Admission and Scholarship Examination (SASE) score—to recommend programs that best align with their skills, interests, and career aspirations.


How it Works

PRIS intelligently matches each student's profile with a curated list of academic programs that reflect their strengths and preferences. The system provides students with:

  • A tailored set of recommended programs, optimized for their individual profiles.
  • Guidance through the curriculum of each recommended program.
  • Insights into potential career pathways associated with each program, aiding students in making informed decisions about their academic and professional futures.

A Commitment to Educational Innovation

    The MSU-IIT PRIS exemplifies the university’s commitment to integrating research and technology into educational guidance. As a one-stop career guidance platform, PRIS reflects MSU-IIT’s dedication to fostering a supportive, inclusive, and efficient learning environment for all students. Through innovations like PRIS, MSU-IIT remains at the forefront of enhancing student success and promoting academic excellence. For more information on MSU-IIT PRIS or to explore potential academic pathways, please contact the Center for Computational Analytics and Modelling at PRISM.
