College Programs Offered

  College of Engineering


  • BSCE - Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

    This program focuses on designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure like bridges and buildings, integrating principles of mathematics and physics.

  • BSEE - Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering

    Students learn about electrical systems, circuits, and devices, preparing for careers in power generation, electronics, and telecommunications.

  • BSMETE - Bachelor of Science Metallurgical Engineering

    This program explores the properties and applications of metals, alloys, and materials, covering extraction, processing, and production techniques.

  • BSCHE - Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering

    Students study chemical processes and materials to develop products like fuels, pharmaceuticals, and plastics, blending chemistry, physics, and engineering principles.

  • BSEsE - Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering

    Focused on electronic systems and devices, this degree covers areas like telecommunications, digital systems, and microelectronics.

  • BSCPE - Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering

    Combining computer science and electrical engineering, this program prepares students to design and develop computer hardware and software.

  • BSME - Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering

    Students learn about mechanical systems and machines, applying principles of physics and materials science to design and analyze devices.

  • BET-CET - Bachelor of Engineering Technology - Civil Engineering Technology

    This program emphasizes the practical application of civil engineering concepts, preparing students for careers in construction management and structural design.

  • BET-MMT - Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Metallurgy and Materials Engineering Technology

    Focused on materials science and engineering, this degree covers areas like materials processing, testing, and quality control.

  • BSEM - Bachelor of Science in Mining Engineering

    Students learn about mining operations, mineral processing, and mine design, preparing for careers in resource extraction and environmental management.

  • BSIAM - Bachelor of Science in Industrial Automation & Mechatronics

    This program integrates mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering for designing automated systems, robots, and manufacturing processes.

  • BET-ELET - Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Electrical Engineering Technology

    Focused on practical applications, this program covers electrical power systems, electronics, and instrumentation for industries like power generation and automation.

  • BET-CHET - Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Chemical Engineering Technology

    This degree focuses on the practical aspects of chemical engineering, preparing students for roles in process control, production, and safety management.

  • BET-ESET - Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Electronics Engineering Technology

    Students learn about electronic circuits, communication systems, and embedded systems design, preparing for roles in telecommunications and consumer electronics.

  • BET-MET - Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Mechanical Engineering Technology

    This program emphasizes hands-on skills in mechanical design, manufacturing, and maintenance, preparing students for careers in industries like automotive and aerospace.

  • BSEnE - Bachelor of Science Environmental Engineering

    This degree focuses on solutions to environmental issues, covering areas like water treatment, air pollution control, and sustainable development.

  • BSCERE - Bachelor of Science in Ceramic Engineering

    Students study the properties and applications of ceramics and related materials, preparing for careers in industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and biomedical engineering.

  • BS EnvET - Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering Technology

    Focused on practical solutions for environmental challenges, this program covers topics like waste management, pollution prevention, and sustainable engineering practices.


  College of Arts and Social Sciences


  • BA POL SCI - Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

    This program explores political theories, institutions, and processes, preparing students for careers in government, international relations, and public policy.

  • BA ELS - Bachelor of Arts in English Language Studies

    Focused on the English language's structure and usage, this degree covers areas like linguistics, literature, and language teaching.

  • BA PSYCH - Bachelor of Arts in Psychology

    Students study human behavior and mental processes, exploring areas such as cognition, emotion, development, and abnormal psychology.

  • BS PSYCH - Bachelor of Science in Psychology

    This program provides a comprehensive understanding of human behavior and mental processes, covering biological, cognitive, emotional, social, and developmental aspects of psychology. Students are prepared for careers in research, healthcare, education, and industry.

  • BS PHIL - Bachelor of Science in Philosophy major in Applied Ethics

    This program delves into philosophical theories and ethical principles, with a focus on their practical application in areas like business, healthcare, and law.

  • BA HISTORY - Bachelor of Arts in History

    This degree explores past events, societies, and cultures, developing critical thinking and research skills valuable in fields such as education, museum curation, and archival work.

  • BA FIL - Batsilyer ng Sining sa Filipino

    Ang kursong ito ay naglalaman ng pag-aaral sa kasaysayan, kultura, at panitikan ng Filipino, handaing ang mga mag-aaral para sa mga karera sa edukasyon, kultura, at midya.

  • BA SOCIO - Bachelor of Arts in Sociology

    Students examine social structures, institutions, and relationships, gaining insights into topics like inequality, culture, and social change. This prepares them for careers in social work, research, and advocacy.

  College of Computer Studies


  • BSIT - Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

    This program focuses on the application of technology to solve business problems, covering areas like software development, network administration, and database management.

  • BSCS - Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

    Students study algorithms, programming languages, and software development, preparing for careers in software engineering, artificial intelligence, and computer systems analysis.

  • BSCA - Bachelor of Science in Computer Applications

    This program emphasizes the development and implementation of computer applications, with a focus on IoT systems. Students learn to design, implement, and manage IoT devices and networks, preparing for careers in IoT development and smart technology integration.

  • BSIS - Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

    Focused on the intersection of technology and business, this program covers topics like data management, systems analysis, and IT project management, preparing students for roles in managing information technology within organizations.

  College of Education


  • BSED BIO - Bachelor of Secondary Education (Biology)

    This program prepares educators to teach biology in secondary schools, covering topics like life sciences, ecology, and genetics, to cultivate a passion for science in students.

  • BSED CHEM - Bachelor of Secondary Education (Chemistry)

    Students in this program learn to teach chemistry concepts and experiments, equipping them to inspire understanding of chemical properties and reactions.

  • BTLED-IA - Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education-Industrial Arts

    Focused on practical skills, this degree prepares educators to teach industrial arts subjects like woodworking, metalworking, and electronics, fostering hands-on learning and technical knowledge.

  • BTVTED-DT - Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education Drafting Technology

    This program trains teachers to instruct in drafting technology, including architectural and engineering drawing techniques, to prepare students for technical design careers.

  • BPED - Bachelor of Physical Education

    Students study human movement, sports science, and fitness education, preparing them for careers as physical education teachers promoting health and wellness.

  • BSED PHYS - Bachelor of Secondary Education (Physics)

    This degree focuses on teaching physics principles, theories, and experiments, enabling educators to engage students in the understanding of natural laws and phenomena.

  • BSED MATH - Bachelor of Secondary Education (Mathematics)

    This program equips future educators with the skills to teach mathematics, covering areas like algebra, geometry, and calculus, to develop problem-solving and analytical thinking in students.

  • BEED SCI MAT - Bachelor of Elementary Education (Science and Mathematics)

    This degree focuses on training elementary school teachers in science and mathematics education, emphasizing foundational concepts and inquiry-based learning methods.

  • BSED FIL - Bachelor of Secondary Education (Filipino)

    This program prepares educators to teach the Filipino language and literature, fostering appreciation for the country's culture and literature among students.

  • BEED Lang Ed - Bachelor of Elementary Education (Language Education)

    Students in this program learn to teach language and literacy skills to elementary students, focusing on effective communication and language development.

  • BTLED-HE - Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education - Home Economics

    This degree focuses on teaching home economics subjects, including cooking, nutrition, family management, and textile arts, to prepare students for careers in homemaking and related fields.

  College of Health Sciences


  • BSNURSING - Bachelor of Science in Nursing

    This program equips students with the knowledge and skills to provide comprehensive nursing care, covering areas such as healthcare ethics, patient assessment, medical treatments, and clinical practice. Graduates are prepared for diverse roles in healthcare settings, from hospitals to community clinics, contributing to patient well-being and promoting health.

  College of Science and Mathematics


  • BSBIO(MCB) - Bachelor of Science in Biology (Microbiology)

    This program focuses on the study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, and fungi, exploring their roles in health, disease, and the environment.

  • BSBIO(AnBio) - Bachelor of Science in Biology (Animal Biology)

    Students in this program study the biology and behavior of animals, covering topics like evolution, physiology, and ecology to understand animal life forms.

  • BSCHEM - Bachelor of Science in Chemistry

    This degree delves into the study of matter, its properties, and chemical reactions, preparing students for careers in research, pharmaceuticals, and environmental analysis.

  • BSPHYSICS - Bachelor of Science in Physics

    Focused on the study of matter, energy, and their interactions, this program covers areas such as mechanics, thermodynamics, and quantum physics, preparing students for careers in research, engineering, and academia.

  • BSBIO(Bdv) - Bachelor of Science in Biology (Biodiversity)

    This program explores the diversity of life forms on Earth, covering taxonomy, conservation, and ecosystem dynamics to understand and preserve biological diversity.

  • BSMATH - Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

    Students in this program study advanced mathematical concepts and theories, preparing them for careers in finance, data analysis, cryptography, and academia.

  • BSBIO(PlBio) - Bachelor of Science in Biology (Plant Biology)

    This degree focuses on the study of plants, including their growth, development, and interactions with the environment, preparing students for careers in agriculture, botany, and environmental science.

  • BSBIO(Marine Bio) - Bachelor of Science in Marine Biology

    Students in this program study marine ecosystems, marine life forms, and oceanic processes, preparing them for careers in marine conservation, aquaculture, and research.

  • BSSTAT - Bachelor of Science in Statistics

    Focused on the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data, this program equips students with statistical methods and tools for decision-making in fields such as business, healthcare, and social sciences.


  College of Economics and Business Administration


  • BSA - Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

    This program prepares students with the knowledge and skills in financial accounting, auditing, taxation, and management accounting, enabling them to pursue careers as accountants or financial analysts in various industries.

  • BS HM - Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

    Students in this program learn about the management and operations of hotels, restaurants, and tourism establishments, focusing on customer service, marketing, and business administration within the hospitality industry.

  • BSBA-MKT MGT - Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (Marketing Management)

    This degree emphasizes marketing strategies, consumer behavior, and market research, preparing students for roles in brand management, advertising, and sales.

  • BS ENTREP - Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

    Students learn to develop and manage their own businesses, covering topics such as business planning, finance, innovation, and risk management to become successful entrepreneurs.

  • BS ECON - Bachelor of Science in Economics

    This program delves into economic theories, policies, and analysis, equipping students with skills in data interpretation and economic decision-making for careers in finance, consulting, or government.

  • BSBA-B.ECON - Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (Business Economics)

    Focused on the intersection of business and economics, this program explores how economic principles affect business decisions, preparing students for roles in market analysis, economic forecasting, and policy analysis.
